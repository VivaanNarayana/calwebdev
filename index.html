About Us

We are a passionate community of student developers dedicated to advancing web development skills and creating real-world impact. Our club brings together aspiring developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts to learn, create, and innovate together.

Learn

Master modern web technologies through hands-on projects and workshops.

Collaborate

Work with fellow students on real-world projects and build an impressive portfolio.

Innovate

Create cutting-edge web applications and explore emerging technologies.

What We Do

Web Development Workshops

Learn web development from the ground up through our structured workshop series. We cover everything from HTML basics to advanced frameworks like React.

  • Coding sessions
  • Hands-on projects
  • Code reviews

Annual Hackathon

Join our exciting 24-hour hackathon where students collaborate to build innovative web solutions. Network with people and win amazing prizes!

  • 24-hour coding challenge
  • Workshops and talks
  • Networking opportunities

Business Solutions

We help local businesses establish their online presence through custom-built websites. Gain real-world experience while making a difference in our community.

  • Custom website development
  • Professional portfolio building
  • Client communication experience
